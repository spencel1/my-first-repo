Pugs!

A short essay by a pug

Pugs are superior to any other domesticated animal. in fact, I would argue the pug is better than other dogs. "Why do you say that?" I can hear you asking me, well look at it, the pug is absolutely majestic in every way possible. In fact in ancient China pugs were bred to be lapdogs to the emperor. Also adding to their majesicness is that pugs are decendents of the mighty bull mastiff after several generations of breeding with smaller dogs.
